Settlers 3D · playable

The world and the whole economy are the 2D game's code, lifted unchanged — production chains, the flag/road carrier network, population & seasons. Only the renderer + controls are new.

Pick a building (left), click a green tile to place it. Carriers walk goods along roads to your HQ. Right-click cancels. R road · X demolish · Space pause · Ctrl-S/L save/load (auto-saves too).
